Lazar Radicevic

I am currently a postdoc at at King's College London. My mentor is Rachel Newton Previously, I was a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics, Laboratoire de Mathématiques Besançon, and at the Mathematical Institute SANU, and before that, I was a PhD student at the University of Cambridge, under the supervision of Tom Fisher.
I am interested in the arithmetic of elliptic curves, local-global principles and arithmetic statistics.
